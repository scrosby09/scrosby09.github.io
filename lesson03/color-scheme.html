WeatherEye Color Scheme


I chose these colors using Blue and Black as the primary contrasts. Blue and Black are excellent because they make text very easy to read organize. Also those two colors are very flexible for dual light and dark mode on any device. Also, the Blue and Black correlate with the imagery of the bright blue sky in daytime, and the dark sky at night, further emphasizing the 24/7 weather forcasting.

The lighter shades help diversify space between elements, and the Yellow incorporates an important pop in color for the eyes.
