Self protection against the Coronavirus COVID-19

By Will Brown

As the world continues to struggle against the Coronavirus, many people have found new and inventive ways to stay safe. Some of the designs are below are just some of the self made DIY methods people hav employed to battle this terrible infection rate. Although crude in their nature the effect is still the same. Keep your distance. One could ask what could that plastic box over your head do to keep you safe from infection. It has been proposed it is not so much the effectiveness of keeping you from infected droplets rather that it forces others to social distance from you. As only a "crazy" person would wear a plastic container on their head. Right now in this environment, perhaps a little crazy would benefit us all or at least give us a laugh.
