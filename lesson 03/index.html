WeatherEye.news Site Plan

Domain

weathereye.newsdomain was found on Google Domains and is priced at $60 per year.

Site Purpose

WeatherEye news aims to present global weather data in a format which closely mimics the UX of Google Maps and Google Earth.

Weather data will be organized by postal area zones. Users will be able select a zone, and like Google Maps when a location or business of interest is selected, multiple weather news articles which are most recent, relevent, and reputable to that zone will populate.
